Slop Surprise

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup expired milk
  • 1 cup canned sardines (including oil)
  • 1 cup overripe bananas
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup crushed stale crackers
  • 1/4 cup melted margarine
  • Salt and pepper to taste
